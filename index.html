👨‍💻 Hi! I am Pooja and this My Journey into Data Science

During my graduation, I stumbled upon the term *data science* in a lecture. At first, I was hooked by the flashy salary figures 💸 and the fact that I’d still get to use a bit of mathematics 📊—after all, that’s what everyone was asking me about: "What’s next after math?" 🤔 (Spoiler: I had no idea back then!)

So, I started digging into data science 🔍, and surprisingly, it wasn’t the salary that held my interest. As I got deeper, I found myself more captivated by its real-world applications 🌐 and the way it seamlessly bridges with mathematics 🧠. In math, I was always focused on the “how” (as in, "how am I supposed to solve this problem?"), but data science handed me something even better—the "why" behind it all 💡.

Now, I’m genuinely enjoying this back-and-forth between math and data science 🔄. Who knew that crunching numbers could answer more than just equations?

Thanks for stopping by! Hope you enjoyed the tour. 🚀 Come back soon – my data isn’t going anywhere! 😉
