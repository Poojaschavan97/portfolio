Power BI Dashboard for Coursera Online Courses


I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Coursera's online courses, focusing on category-wise insights to guide content development. The dashboard provides:

- Course Distribution: Tracks the number of courses by category & sub-category.
- Viewer Engagement: Analyzes average views by category, sub-category, and language.
- Trending Skills: Identifies popular skills per category.
- Language Insights: Examines language distribution and top category preferences.
- Subtitle Impact: Evaluates how subtitles influence views.
- Top Instructors: Highlights the best-rated educators per category.
- Course Duration & Views: Studies engagement based on course length.
- Skill Variety & Viewership: Assesses the impact of diverse skills on engagement.

This dashboard enables data-driven decisions for optimizing recorded lecture offerings and audience reach.
